  Did you know?
  The Model number mentioned in the size of frame indicates different parts of frame measurement. First of the series indicates your eye size, second the bridge size and third indicates Temple size.
For instance, if model no. is 54-20-140, then eye size is 54, bridge size is 20 and temple length is 140 (in mm).
