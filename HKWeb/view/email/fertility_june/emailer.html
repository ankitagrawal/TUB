FACTS ABOUT INFERTILITY
Infertility is one's inability to conceive a child despite aggressive effort. According to reports, around 100 million couples worldwide want to have a child, of which 17% experience trouble in the same.
Couples in the age group 20-33 have about 20-25% chance of conceiving a child. These chances decrease with age.

Infertility can arise due to equal probability of problems with both genders.
In women, imbalance of sex hormones cause infertility. It is known as PCOS.
Endometriosis is also one condition in women which makes cells from uterus grow in other areas of body, thus creating irregular bleeding, and hindering conception.
In men, Oligiospermia is a condition where semen has low sperm concentration and azoospermia is a condition where men face ejaculation disorders.
